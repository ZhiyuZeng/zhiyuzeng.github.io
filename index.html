Zhiyu Zeng

Hi, I am a Post-Doctoral Research Associate at the Olin Business School, Washington University in St. Louis.

My research examines operational challenges within digital platforms, adopting a causality-centric lens. I utilize data-driven causal inference, field experimentation, structural modeling, and machine learning techniques to analyze and enhance the strategies within online marketplaces. A particular focus of my work is on improving the incentives for both supply and demand sides of these digital ecosystems.
